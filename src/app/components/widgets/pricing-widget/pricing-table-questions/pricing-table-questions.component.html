General Questions
Is there a setup fee?
Absolutely Not! There are ZERO setup fees.
Can I cancel my account at any time?
Yes. Please read the terms and conditions for more information.
Am I required to sign a contract?
Nope! Each plan is month to month *unless you select a flat fee (non-maintenance) plan.
Can I change my plan later on?
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.
Can I use my own domain name?
Yes you may! You can choose to either register a domain name with us or bring your own. Your choice.
 
What are your bandwidth fees?
There are no bandwidth restrictions for your website.
Do I need a web host?
You do not need a web host. Similar to domains, you are more than welcomed to use your personal host.
Which Content Management Systems do you offer?
You have the option of selecting virtually any CMS you woud like to work with. The most popular solutions include Wordpress, Joomla, Magento, and Shopify. A CMS is not a requirement. We also build websites with Pure JavaScript, Angular, React, and Vue.
